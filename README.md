# Chereka Technology

**Innovating Africa’s Digital Future**

Chereka Technology is a technology company based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, focused on building intelligent, scalable, and business-ready software solutions. We combine artificial intelligence, cloud-native engineering, and modern product design to deliver platforms that solve real-world problems across logistics, education, finance, and enterprise operations.

---

## Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading AI-driven technology company, empowering enterprises with intelligent systems that enhance efficiency, security, and growth.

## Our Mission

To deliver high-value digital solutions through innovation, automation, and deeply localized technology that reflects the needs of African markets.

---

## What We Build

### 1. Intelligent Software Platforms
- End-to-end SaaS systems for logistics, delivery, fleet management, and operations.
- Smart workflow automation and enterprise management tools.

### 2. AI & Machine Learning Solutions
- Natural Language Processing (Amharic & multilingual)
- Predictive analytics, forecasting, and data intelligence
- Computer vision and edge-AI solutions
- Intelligent RAG systems and domain-specific LLMs

### 3. Mobile & Web Applications
- Modern, responsive interfaces built with cutting-edge frameworks.
- Mobile-first applications tailored for African connectivity conditions.

### 4. Custom Enterprise Systems
- School management systems (SMS)
- HR, finance, inventory, and operations systems
- Full-stack product development for businesses and NGOs
